Savings

Savings refer to the portion of income that individuals set aside for future use rather than spending it immediately. Saving is a fundamental financial habit that serves various purposes, including building an emergency fund, achieving specific financial goals, and providing a safety net for unexpected expenses. By consistently saving a portion of their income, individuals can create a financial cushion that helps them navigate financial challenges and pursue opportunities without relying on debt or external assistance. There are several methods and strategies for saving money effectively. One common approach is to establish a budget that outlines income, expenses, and savings goals, allowing individuals to allocate funds towards savings systematically.
